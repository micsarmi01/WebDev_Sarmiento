What is the Agile Software Development Methodology?

The main theme with Agile development is the ability to adapt and quickly. Agile developement expects change and many changes. Agile depends on many iterations and many adaptations. This specific methodology puts out software as soon as it is viable and relies on the feedback to make changes to the product.














Here are some key features that make the Agile process a good choice

  • Collaboration promoted – Team work and collaboration is promoted, this aids in the flexability of the agile method
  • Client communication and feedback – Agile development requires constant feedback to mold the product to the exact desires of the client
  • Flexible – Agile development embraces change

Here are some negative features of the agile Process

  • Minimal Structure – The agile process has minimal structure which can lead to sometimes low productivity
  • Dependent upon personel – A project can fall apart because of lack of documentation and reliance on personel
  • Time consuming – a high demand for collaboration and feedback can be long processes
